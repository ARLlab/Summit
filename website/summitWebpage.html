    • This site presents preliminary results of atmospheric near-real time monitoring at GEOSummit in Greenland. The monitoring is conducted by researchers at the Institute of Arctic and Alpine Research (INSTAAR) at the University of Colorado. Atmospheric data is gathered from a Picarro instrument and from a GC-FID instrument.
